Amazing lion facts
Lions are the most sociable of all the big cats. They live in groups called prides, which usually consist of related females and their offspring
Lions can go up to four days without drinking water, but if available, they will drink water every day. Lions do need to eat every day
Lionesses are the primary hunters of the pride. They are smaller and more agile than males and they use teamwork to bring an animal down. After a successful hunt, all the lions in the pride share the meal
Although lions are known as the "king of the jungle", lions in Africa do not actually live in a jungle. Instead, their primary habitats consist of Africa’s grasslands and plains
